About

A concise place for ideas on AI, agents, and systems thinking — and the person writing about them.

The Bot Layer is a newsletter created by Terel Lewis for CAH-205 that focuses on all aspects of agentic AI. From product design to the evolution of commerce, emerging technology has the potential to transform industries and improve efficiency tenfold. Follow our journey to learn more about agentic AI as it continues to evolve into an adaptive, goal-oriented tool that assists individuals and organizations in completing their tasks.
