The Retail Evolution

January 14, 2026

Retail is changing. The interplay of digital experiences, logistics optimization, and adaptive agentic systems is remaking how customers discover and receive value.

Customer experience as product

Today the product is not just what you buy — it's how you discover, customize, and receive it. Stores and platforms that succeed treat discovery and delivery as first-class product features.

Supply & flow

Inventory becomes a fluid system when visibility, orchestration, and agent-assisted decisioning reduce friction between demand and supply.

What comes next

Expect tighter coordination between intent signals and fulfillment, richer personal experiences, and an increased role for agentic systems that can act on behalf of customers and businesses.
